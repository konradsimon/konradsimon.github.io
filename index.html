Konrad Simon

Hi, my name is Konrad! I am a media artist, musician and producer. I am currently studying at the Institute for Music and Media in Düsseldorf. My focus lies in the visualization of music, where I like to combine my musical versatility with innovative approaches in media art. My latest audiovisual work "manipulation" explores the influence of humans on complex algorithms through targeted manipulation.
