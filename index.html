Konrad Simon


Storytelling through sound and visuals. At the Institute for Music and Media in Düsseldorf, I’m exploring new ways to turn sound into something you can see, feel, and experience. I love creating immersive visual worlds that expand the musical experience. By blending my diverse musical background with innovative media art, I push the boundaries of how we perceive sound.Take "Manipulation", for example—my latest audiovisual project. It explores the fine line between human interaction and the precision of algorithms, showing how we shape, twist, and redefine the systems we build. Let’s dive into the space where sound and vision collide.
