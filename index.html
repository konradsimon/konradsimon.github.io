Konrad Simon

At the Institute for Music and Media in Düsseldorf, I study where sound and image intersect—exploring how audio can become a visual experience. My projects blend a broad musical background with experimental media art, focusing on how technology transforms the way we perceive and interact with sound. I work primarily with TouchDesigner, using it to craft real-time audiovisual experiences that respond to human, algorythmic and musical inputs. I explore the relationship between sound, space, and perception—pushing the boundaries of what new media art can be. Feel free to get in touch—I’m always open to new ideas and collaborations.
